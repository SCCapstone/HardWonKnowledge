Using the Application

Login Screen

Upon opening the application on an iPad device, you will see the login screen. By entering in a correct username/password combination into the appropriate fields and hitting the "Log In" button, further access will be given to you.

Bookshelf

Once logged in (if you aren't an administrator), you will see the bookshelf of notebooks. On the initial startup of the application, the bookshelf will look like the image provided; however, the fields will eventually fill up with notebooks as more are created. To create a new notebook simply click the Create New rectangle which will launch you to the notebook portion of the app.

Notebook

When creating a new notebook, the provided blank white screen with the gray side and bottom bars will display. Simply press the buttons on the side to achieve your desired goal.

Paint

If you wish to draw in your notebook, the Paint button will give you access to free hand. Press the color that you wish to you (the selected color will have a star in its box) and then select the size of the brush (small, medium, or large). Click in the white space to begin drawing. Press the Erase button to erase what you want to remove.

Insert Text

Pressing the Text button will let you put text into your notebook. Simply type in your comment into the textfield, press the Create button, and then tap anywhere in the white screen that you want the text to go. Press the Erase button to erase what you want to remove.

Insert Photo

If you want to insert a picture into the notebook, click the Camera button. This will give you the option of either importing a picture or taking a new picture. Then you would simply do as you did with the text feature, and tap anywhere in the white screen where you want the picture to be place. Press the Erase button to erase what you want to remove.

Administrator Features

Administrator Login Features

If the account being logged into is an administrator account, the login will send the user to the screen on your left where other options are available:
  • STEM Notebook - This button opens the notebooks display on the user's bookshelf.
  • User Settings - This button allows the administrator user to view and edit users of this application.
  • Google Drive Connectivity - This button allows the administrator user to connect or disconnect from Google Drive on the device.
  • Notebook Log Out - This button allows the user to log out of the notebook and returns to the original screen
