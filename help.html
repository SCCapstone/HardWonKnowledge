Helpful Tips

The use of this application is essentially pretty simple and easy. Provided below are instructional demonstrations of how to work the notebook most efficiently.

Help Video

Watch the video for a demonstration of how to work this application.

Using the Application

Login Screen

When you open the application on an iPad device, you will see the login screen. By entering in a correct username/password combination into the appropriate fields and hitting the "Log In" button, further access will be given to you.

Bookshelf

Once logged in (if you aren't an administrator), you will see the bookshelf of notebooks. On the initial startup of the application, the bookshelf will look like the image provided; however, the fields will eventually fill up with notebooks as more are created. To create a new notebook simply click the Create New rectangle which will launch you to the notebook portion of the app.

Notebook

When creating a new notebook, the provided blank white screen with the gray side and bottom bars will display. Simply press the buttons on the side to achieve your desired goal.

Paint

If you wish to draw in your notebook, the Paint button will give you access to free hand. Press the color that you wish to you (the selected color will have a star in its box) and then select the size of the brush (small, medium, or large). Click in the white space to begin drawing. Press the Erase button to erase what you want to remove.

Insert Text

Pressing the Text button will let you put text into your notebook. Simply type in your comment into the textfield, press the Create button, and then tap anywhere in the white screen that you want the text to go. Press the Erase button to erase what you want to remove.

Insert Photo

If you want to insert a picture into the notebook, click the Camera button. This will give you the option of either importing a picture or taking a new picture. Then you would simply do as you did with the text feature, and tap anywhere in the white screen where you want the picture to be place. Press the Erase button to erase what you want to remove.

Changing Notebook Page

If you want to change to a different page of the notebook, just press the arrows provided near the bottom of the darker gray side bar. Each notebook contains 25 pages.

Saving

When you are finish with your notebook entries, press the Done button on the sidebar. A prompt will show (for new notebooks only) that will ask you to name your newly created notebook. Press Okay to save the notebook.
The screen will then switch to the bookshelf again, where you will see your newly added notebook. Green notebooks mean that the file has been uploaded safely into the class' account, and black notebooks mean the file is saved on the device but hasn't been able to upload into the class' account.

Administrator Features

As an administrator, certain features are needed to help regulate the use of the application by other users. Provided are features that aid the administrator in this pursuit.

Login Features

After you log into the login screen, you will see a new screen that will give you options of where you wish to proceed.

STEM Notebook

This button opens the bookshelf, much like the previously mentioned display; however, all user notebooks will be shown as well as the user's notebooks.

User Settings

This button allows the administrator user to view and edit users of this application. There are options to add, update, and remove users. Using these features are fairly simple, as you just follow the directions.



Google Drive Connectivity

This button allows the administrator user to connect or disconnect from Google Drive on the device. Simply click the button and, depending on whether you are connected or not, an appropriate option will be given to you. Log In to Google Drive with your account information.

Notebook Log Out

This button allows the user to log out of the notebook and returns to the original screen for another user to input their credentials.

Administrator Bookshelf Feature

Delete

The trash can icon in the bottom left of the screen (in the image to your left) allows the administrator to remove notebooks from the screen. Just click the icon and a list of notebooks will show (image to your right), much like the user's View/Edit list, and select the notebook that you wish to remove.
