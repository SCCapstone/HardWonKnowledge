About Us

This app has been made possible by the hard work of five individuals coming together, over nine months, with the one goal of creating an ideal notebook for use in a modern classroom. We strive to introduce innovation on aimed towards both students and teachers to help enhance studies of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
